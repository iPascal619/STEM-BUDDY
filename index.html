Are the courses on STEM Buddy self-paced?

Absolutely! You can learn at your own pace with STEM Buddy. Take your time to explore each lesson, try out experiments, and play games at a pace that suits you best. Learning should be fun and stress-free!

Can parents or teachers track my progress on STEM Buddy?

Yes! Parents and teachers can create accounts on STEM Buddy to monitor their children's or students' progress. Track completed lessons, view quiz scores, and see where your child excels or needs extra support.

Are there any costs associated with using STEM Buddy?

Nope! STEM Buddy is completely free to use. We believe that quality STEM education should be accessible to everyone, so there are no hidden fees or subscription charges. Just dive in and start learning!

How can I get help if I have questions?

If you have any questions or need assistance while using STEM Buddy, you can reach out to our friendly support team. Simply send us an email or message through our contact page, and we'll be happy to help you out!
